This project applies core computer networking theory to a simulated technology company. The topology implements **OSPF dynamic routing**, **EtherChannel** link aggregation, **Spanning Tree Protocol** for loop prevention, **VPN** tunneling, and **VLAN** segmentation across web and internal services — a hands-on rehearsal of how enterprise networks are actually designed and secured.
