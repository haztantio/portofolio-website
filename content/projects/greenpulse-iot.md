GreenPulse is a project I'm currently building to monitor plant health in real time. It combines several environmental sensors with an **ESP32** microcontroller, streaming live readings to the **Blynk** app so plant conditions can be checked from anywhere — turning a simple environmental-care idea into a small, practical piece of connected technology.
